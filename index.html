FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to the questions we hear most often from clients across Mumbai, Pune and beyond.

What services does Sardigi Prints offer?+
We offer a complete range of printing, signage and branding services — acrylic letters & displays, LED and glow signboards, eco solvent / UV digital printing, vehicle branding, laser cutting, sunboard & vinyl printing, retail floor branding, and customized corporate gifting, all under one roof.
Which cities do you deliver to?+
We are headquartered in Mumbai with active branches in Pune, Nashik, Vadodara, Ahmedabad and Bangalore, and we regularly execute projects for clients across India.
How do I get a price quote for my order?+
Simply share your requirement — service type, size and quantity — through our Upload Artwork form, the Contact form below, or directly on WhatsApp. Our team typically responds with a detailed quote within 24 hours.
What file formats can I send for printing?+
We accept PDF, AI, PSD, CDR, EPS, JPG and PNG files. For large files over 10MB, you can share a Google Drive or Dropbox link, or simply send the file to us directly on WhatsApp or email.
What is your typical turnaround time?+
Turnaround depends on the service and order volume, but we pride ourselves on fast execution without compromising quality. Standard orders are usually ready within a few business days — share your deadline with us and we'll confirm timelines upfront.
Do you handle bulk and corporate orders?+
Yes. We regularly work with national retail chains and corporate clients on bulk signage, store branding and gifting orders, with dedicated support and competitive pricing for larger volumes.
Is my design file kept confidential?+
Absolutely. We guarantee secure and confidential handling of every file and print job you share with us.
How can I contact your team directly?+
You can call or WhatsApp us at +91 9870752589, email sardigiprints@gmail.com, or use the contact form below — whichever is most convenient for you.
